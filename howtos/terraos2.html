Getting into the Boot Menu

With your flashed usb drive, power on your chromebook and press the following buttons at once:

Esc, Reload, Power

You should be brought to a screen asking you to plug in a recovery usb, instead press Ctrl+D.

On this screen, press escape reload power once more. If you return to the same screen, try again.

Once you see the screen below you may select "Boot a partition" and select the terraos partition

Congrats, you have booted terraos! Continue to Terra OS setup
